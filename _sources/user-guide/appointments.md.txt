# Appointments

The **Appointments** tab shows all your scheduled and past clinical visits. You can view details, prepare for upcoming appointments, and review post-visit summaries.

---

## Viewing Upcoming Appointments

1. Tap the **Appointments** tab.
2. The **Upcoming** section lists all future appointments sorted by date.
3. Each appointment card shows:
   - Provider name and specialty
   - Date, time, and location
   - A countdown ("in 3 days")
   - Appointment type (e.g. "Follow-up", "Annual Check", "Specialist Referral")

---

## Appointment Details

Tap any appointment card to open its detail view:

- **Provider** -- full name, designation, and clinic
- **When and where** -- date, time, facility name, and address
- **Reason for visit** -- the stated purpose on the appointment
- **Pre-Visit Summary** -- an AI-generated brief of your health status (see below)
- **What to bring** -- a checklist of documents, referral letters, or fasting requirements if applicable

---

## Pre-Visit Summary

For appointments scheduled at least 24 hours in advance, the AI generates a **Pre-Visit Summary** automatically. It appears in the appointment detail view under the **AI Summary** section and includes:

- A plain-language overview of your health since the last visit
- Medications you are currently on (and any adherence gaps)
- Recent biometric trends
- Questions suggested by the AI for you to raise with your doctor

---

## Appointment Reminders

You will receive push notifications:

- **48 hours before** -- reminder with appointment details
- **1 hour before** -- final reminder

To adjust notification timing, go to **Settings > Appointment Reminders**.

---

## Past Appointments

Scroll down or tap **Past** to see completed visits:

- Encounter date and provider
- Visit notes (if your care team has shared them)
- Any follow-up instructions
- Post-visit AI summary (where available)

---

## Requesting Appointments

Currently, new appointment requests are handled directly through your clinic. Use the **Chat** tab to contact your care team, or call your clinic directly.
